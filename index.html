GestureLink is an easy tool to use for deaf and dumb people to communicate to others. The product as shown in the image is a glove that uses motion sensors to translate sign language into audible speech. This can provide for quick and easy communication between the deaf, dumb and speaking people.

The glove comes in various sizes and can be customised according the the user's liking. The prize range varies from INR 1000 to INR 1500
